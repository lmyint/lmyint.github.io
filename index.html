About Writing Teaching Talks

Hi, I'm Leslie Myint

And I find joy in stories.

My storytelling media of choice are data and games.

As an Assistant Professor of Statistics at Macalester College, I’m driven by telling causal stories, particularly about biology and public health. I’m also passionate about teaching my students to cultivate their data-storytelling skills.

As a gamer (board, console, tabletop, you name it!), I’m fascinated by the interplay between game structure and story.
